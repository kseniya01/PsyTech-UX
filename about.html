Hello, I'm Xenia Lechler

As a growing UX specialist with a unique blend of expertise in psychology and computer science, I bring a multifaceted perspective to the field of user experience. Currently completing my master's program in user experience, I am passionate about integrating principles from psychology and computer science to create intuitive and user-centric digital experiences. Human-centered mission statement

My experience in IRB research and human subjects research encompasses a comprehensive understanding of ethical considerations and protocols governing research involving human participants. With a background in statistical analysis, I've applied rigorous methodologies to ensure the integrity and validity of research outcomes. This expertise extends to designing studies, obtaining IRB approvals, recruiting participants, conducting data collection, and analyzing results. My commitment to ethical research practices and attention to detail ensures the protection of participants' rights and the quality of research outcomes. In my role as a UX researcher, I bring a wealth of experience in designing and conducting user-centered research studies to enhance digital experiences. With a deep understanding of human behavior and interaction design principles, I navigate the intricacies of user research methodologies, from crafting insightful research questions to executing usability tests and gathering actionable insights. My proficiency in qualitative and quantitative analysis equips me to uncover patterns and trends, driving informed design decisions that prioritize user needs and preferences. Moreover, my dedication to ethical research practices ensures the protection of user privacy and the ethical treatment of research participants throughout the study process.
